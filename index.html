About Me

Hi, I’m Sofia Reyes, a third-year Computer Science student studying in Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation. I love creating, and I am currently working on my skills to build creative and functional web applications that can benefit the world. I also do graphic designs as a hobby, letting my creativity flow through my works.

When not in my school uniform, I’m a passionate, poignant poet and lover of art who romanticizes every beautiful fragment of existence on Earth and writes about them as lovely as the way they are.
